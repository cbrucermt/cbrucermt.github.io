Welcome

Hi, my name is Colleen and I graduated from the West Coast College of Massage Therapy in Victoria, BC. in December of 2009.

As a massage therapist, I've worked with all sorts of clients - athletes, pregnant women, office workers, nurses, stroke patients and people with arthritis.

RMT

Registered Massage Therapists (RMTs) in British Columbia are health care professionals committed to restoring and maintaining optimal health and pain-free function of the body.

They are educated and trained to accurately assess and treat with techniques that include massage and manual therapy, joint mobilization, hydrotherapy, and rehabilitative exercise such as stretching, strengthening, postural exercise and patient education.

Why?

Massage Therapy is an effective approach to pain management and rehabilitation. RMTs are effective in treating and providing relief for a wide range of conditions such as migraine headaches, tendonitis, arthritis, fibromyalgia, sports injuries, as well as many other common conditions related to soft tissue and joint dysfunction.

Services

I offer a wide range of techniques including trigger point therapy, muscle energy, myofascial release, Active Isolated Stretching (AIS), Swedish and deep tissue massage.
